Data Deletion Instructions – Bnei Sakhnin App

Last updated: 26 Sep 2025

How to request deletion

  1. Email us at bnei.sakhnin.91@gmail.com with subject “Data Deletion Request”.
  2. Include your account email/ID and any relevant details to locate your data.

What we delete

  • Account profile data (name, email) and associated identifiers.
  • App-related content linked to your account, unless retention is required by law.

Timeline

We aim to confirm and complete deletion within 30 days of a verified request.

Notes

  • We may retain minimal logs if legally required (e.g., fraud prevention).
  • If you signed in via Facebook/Apple/Google, you may also need to revoke access in those services.

Questions? Email: bnei.sakhnin.91@gmail.com
